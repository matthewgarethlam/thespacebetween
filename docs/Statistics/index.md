# Stats shouldn't be scary!

I used to be really bad at maths. At school I would often get pretty bad grades for anything mathsy (including Physics). That changed when I went to university though, and I was shown how maths, statistics and logic can be used to solve real world problems! 

Suddently statistics wasn't just about formulas and equaitons. It was about answering interesting questions, making predictions and understanding how the world works. 

Everyone's journey through stats and maths is different. I certainly took a while to get to grips with fundamental concepts. This section of my blog contains some explainers and tutorials on fundamental stats concepts I wish I had whilst studying. I find it helpful myself to dumb things down and use analogies to help me understand things. 

Hopefully this will help you along your statistical journey too!
